How to Play

🐍

Movement

Arrow Keys or WASD to move one tile at a time. Navigate the winding dungeon corridors.

⚔️

Combat

Walk into an enemy to engage. Attack twice to charge Fang Strike for big damage.

🛡️

Defend

When enemy attacks: DODGE window (2s, easy) → then PARRY window (1.3s, very hard).

📦

Chests

Open chests for stat upgrades. Choose +Max HP or +Attack.

🔥

Traps

Spike traps block your path. Hit a hard PARRY window to pass safely.

🔰

Checkpoints

Restore full HP and save progress. Respawn here if you fall.

🗡️

Mini-Bosses

Each chapter ends with a powerful guardian. Defeat it to unlock the gate.

🌀

The Gate

Reach the swirling gate after defeating the mini-boss to complete the chapter.
